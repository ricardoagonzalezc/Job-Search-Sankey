# Job-Search-Sankey

This was a small project to help visualize and orgainze my job search during my senior year of university. Below you will find a short explanation about Sankey diagrams as well as some good uses. 

These types of diagrams are used to visualize data in which the width of the arrows is proportional to the flow rate of the depicted property, in my case the amount of job applications, interviews, offers etc. The arrows are connected to nodes and each connection is called a link. 

These types of diagrams are also commonly used to visualize energy use, material flow, election results, budget use, cost breakdowns, etc. They emphasize the transfer or flow within a system, which helps visualize the data.
